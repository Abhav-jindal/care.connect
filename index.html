Cardiology

Dedicated to providing expert care for your heart with the latest innovations and compassionate support.

Our cardiology department offers cutting-edge diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects. We work closely with you to improve heart health and enhance quality of life.

Neurology

Specialized in diagnosing and treating complex conditions of the brain, spine, and nervous system.

Our neurology team provides expert care for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson’s disease, multiple sclerosis, and chronic migraines. Using advanced imaging and therapies, we are committed to restoring neurological health and function.

Hepatology

Providing advanced care for liver, gallbladder, and pancreatic disorders with a patient-centered approach.

Our hepatology specialists diagnose and treat liver diseases such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, and liver cancers. We combine cutting-edge treatments with compassionate care to manage and improve liver health.

Pediatrics

Comprehensive medical care for children, from newborns to adolescents, in a safe and nurturing environment.

The pediatrics department focuses on preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment of childhood illnesses and developmental conditions. Our experienced team ensures your child’s physical, emotional, and developmental health at every stage.

Eye Care

Committed to protecting and enhancing your vision with advanced diagnostic and surgical expertise.

Our ophthalmology specialists provide comprehensive eye care, including treatments for cataracts, glaucoma, macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and vision correction procedures. We prioritize preserving and improving your eyesight with the latest technology.

Dermatology

Expert care for skin, hair, and nail conditions to help you look and feel your best.

Our dermatology department provides diagnosis and treatment for acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin infections, hair loss, and skin cancers. We combine clinical expertise with the latest cosmetic and medical dermatology advancements to enhance skin health and appearance.
